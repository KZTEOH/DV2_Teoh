World Happiness Report

In a world constantly chasing success and wealth, the pursuit of happiness stands as the most coveted treasure. It's the elusive treasure we all seek, transcending cultural, geographic, and individual boundaries. But what exactly is happiness, and where do we find it?

In this visualisation, you can have an overview the state of global happiness, identify the contribution of happiness in different region in the world, and discover the relationship between different happiness attributes - economic production, social support, life expectancy, freedom, absence of corruption, and generosity.


Where is Happiness?

The maps shows gives the happiness score of 159 countries around the world based on seven factors including family, life expectancy, economy, generosity, trust in government, freedom, and dystopia residual. Sum of the value of these seven factors gives us the happiness score and the higher the happiness score, the lower the happiness rank. So, it is evident that the higher value of each of these seven factors means the level of happiness is higher.

From this map you can see happiness leaders regions - North America, Western Europe, Australia and New Zealand. Colors from this map reflects the level of happiness.
